Teaching Assistant (2018-2022)

Naval Academy, France

I am currently involved in diverse Computer Science courses for the officers-engineer students (equivalent to 3rd year BSc - 1st year MSc). These courses represent a total volume of 96 hours/year of tutorials and practical courses. A summary of the courses subjects is presented as follows:

Algoritmic and programming: Algorithmic concepts (in C) and Object Oriented Programming (in C++).
Practical courses in algorithmic since 2018 (4*12 hours)
Practical courses C++ in 2019, 2020, 2021 (3*15 hours)
Spatio-temporal database: This course covers the problems related to managing, processing and visualising geolocalised data, as well as the principles of spatio-temporal DB. The module is applied to the monitoring of maritime navigation, along with datascience techniques.
Practical courses in 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021 (4*12 hours)
Databases and Web: Concepts, manipulation, and querying relational databases, along with methods allowing linking web services with databases.
Practical courses in 2019 (16 hours)
Information system & Computer Architecture: General principles of computer architecture, operating systems and information systems functionalities.
Practical courses in 2021 (12 hours)
Wi-fi Networks and Security: Principles on how to establish wifi networks in infrastructure, ad-hoc, and monitor modes. In a second part, authentication and encryption systems (WEP, WPA, Mac Filtering) are studied.
Practical courses since 2018 (4*21 hours)
Networks and Cybersecurity: general concepts on network protocols. Introduction to techniques related to security attacks and network vulnerabilities.
Practical courses in 2019, 2020, 2021 (4*12 hours)
Virtualisation and containerisation: introduction to cloud computing, virtual machines and containers. Principles on how to create virtual machines and Docker containers.
Practical courses in 2019, 2020 (30 + 12 hours)
